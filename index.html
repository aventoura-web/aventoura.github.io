Overview

An Expedition Like
No Other

This carefully curated 10-day journey takes you through the dramatic landscapes of the Himalayas — glaciers, rhododendron forests, ancient trading posts, and ridge-top monasteries where monks have meditated for centuries. Our small-group format ensures a deeply personal experience, with expert local guides who know every hidden trail and sunrise viewpoint.

Whether you're a seasoned trekker or a curious first-timer, this expedition is thoughtfully paced to challenge and inspire without exhausting. Nights are spent in boutique mountain lodges with warm meals, hot showers, and unforgettable panoramas framed in your window.

Day by Day

Your Journey Unfolded

Every day of this expedition is thoughtfully curated to balance exhilarating adventure with genuine rest and cultural immersion.

1
Day 1
Arrival in Kathmandu — Gateway to the Himalaya
Land at Tribhuvan International Airport where your Aventoura guide will welcome you. Transfer to your boutique hotel in Thamel with time to acclimatize. An evening orientation dinner with the team, and your first taste of traditional Nepali cuisine.
TransferWelcome DinnerBriefing
2
Day 2
Kathmandu Valley — Ancient Temples & Stupas
A guided tour through Kathmandu's UNESCO World Heritage Sites. Walk around the magnificent Boudhanath Stupa, explore the living goddess shrine at Kumari Ghar, and wander Swayambhunath at dusk. Afternoon free to explore local markets.
SightseeingUNESCO SitesPhotography
3
Day 3
Fly to Lukla — Trek Begins at Phakding
An exhilarating mountain flight to Lukla Airport, carved into a Himalayan hillside at 2,860m. Trek through rhododendron and pine forests to Phakding village, crossing the Dudhkoshi River on suspension bridges adorned with prayer flags.
Mountain FlightTrek 3–4h2,610m Altitude
4
Day 4
Trek to Namche Bazaar — The Sherpa Capital
A memorable ascent through dense forests to the crescent-shaped mountain town of Namche at 3,440m. If the sky is clear, catch your first glimpse of Everest above the ridge. Explore the Saturday bazaar where Tibetan traders set up their stalls.
Trek 5–6h3,440mSherpa Culture
5
Day 5
Acclimatization Day — Everest View Hike
Rest day in Namche with an acclimatization hike to the Everest View Hotel at 3,880m — the world's highest situated hotel — for stunning panoramas of Everest, Lhotse, and Ama Dablam before descending back to Namche.
Rest DayAcclimatizationPanoramic Views
6–8
Days 6–8
Tengboche · Dingboche · Lobuche — The High Himalaya
Three days of progressive high-altitude trekking through increasingly dramatic landscapes. Visit the iconic Tengboche Monastery with its magnificent Everest backdrop, cross the high pastures of Dingboche, and reach the stone houses of Lobuche at 4,940m.
Trek 4–6h/day4,940m MaxMonastery Visit
9
Day 9
Kala Patthar — The Iconic Sunrise Summit
Rise at 4am for the pre-dawn push to Kala Patthar (5,545m), the iconic viewpoint directly across from Everest. Watch the world's highest peak turn from shadow to gold as the sun rises. The moment every trekker remembers for a lifetime.
5,545m SummitSunrise TrekEverest Views
10
Day 10
Descent & Farewell — Fly Back to Kathmandu
Descend to Lukla for a final celebratory dinner with your guides and fellow adventurers. Morning flight back to Kathmandu where airport transfers await. Carry home something far more valuable than any souvenir.
DescentFarewell DinnerDeparture
